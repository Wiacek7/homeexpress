Tip 1

Select the right box, based on its size and the amount of items you are packing. Do not extend the height of the box, or overstuff it.

Tip 2

Secure all breakable and easily-damaged items in bubble wrap and pack them in the center of the box.

Tip 3

Place liquids, powdered goods and small bolts (screws nuts, etc.) in individual sealed containers and note these are only allowed via sea cargo.

Tip 4

Ensure all sharp items are padded.

Tip 5

Pack greases amd strongly-scented items in durable tins or containers sealed with heavy-duty adhesive.

Tip 6

Empty spaces within the box should be filled with crumpled newspaper or styrofoam peanuts to secure all items.

Tip 7

List down ALL items to be sent. Do not sneak undeclared items.

Tip 8

Apply a "this side up" sticker to avoid mishandling and ensure all seams and flaps are sealed using double layers of packing tape.
